Our Story

Meet the founder - Johnny Boy Wheatland

MyShine was born from Johnny Wheatland's passion for smart, simple living.
Tired of clunky systems and disconnected apps, Johnny set out to create a seamless way for people to control their home lighting from one central place.
With MyShine, users can adjust lights from anywhere—whether they're setting the mood for a cozy evening or making sure everything’s off while they’re away.
It’s lighting, made easy.
